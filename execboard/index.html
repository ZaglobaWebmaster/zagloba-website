Meet The Zagłoba Executive Board

For the 2017-2018 school year

Nicole Pudło

President

Nicole is a Junior majoring in Spanish Education with minors in English and Slavic Studies.

"My favorite memory from Zagłoba is by far the First Annual Cultural Showcase back in December 2017. It was an incredible experience to see our club members grow through participating in the folk dancing group, Christmas carol singing groups, and instrumental performances, as well as bond with fellow members over an aspect of Polish culture which is so graceful, meaningful, and close to our hearts."

Theresa Komperda

Treasurer

Theresa is a Senior majoring in Accounting and minoring in Communications.

"My favorite Polish tradition is Wigilia. This is the one time during the year when the whole family comes together at the dinner table for a delicious meal and great company. Since I've been away at college this is the one holiday I look forward to most."
