The Annual Zagłoba Barn Dance

The BIGGEST Barn Dance at The University of Illinois

Barn Dance is Zagłoba Polish Club’s staple charity event of the year, being the biggest dance at the University of Illinois. The event consists of hayrides, kielbasa and chleb around a bonfire, Polish/dance music DJed by artists from the local and Chicagoland area, and dancing for hours on end into the night!

Proceeds of the event go towards Polish institutions in Chicago and Poland. Charities include: Polish American Association, Dar Serca, foster homes in Poland, and many other organizations and causes.

Walentynki for the Dar Serca foundation in Chicago Dom pod Kasztanami in Siedlce, Poland

Zagłoba Polish Club welcomes all donations towards this event, including audio equipment, performing experience, food and drink, printing services, etc. Past sponsors include: Montrose Deli, Funky Entertainment, Northstar, and other local services.
